Curated galleries — highest signal-to-noise

- Godly (https://godly.website) — probably the best single source right now for genuinely beautiful, unusual sites. Curated by taste, not popularity.
- Awwwards (https://awwwards.com) — longer-running, more well-known, slightly more maximalist/award-bait tendency but still excellent
- Siteinspire (https://siteinspire.com) — older, calmer curation, skews toward the clean and typographic. Good for the Flowershow register specifically.
- Httpster (https://httpster.net) — smaller, quirkier, good for finding things off the beaten path

More specific:

- Minimal Gallery (https://minimal.gallery) — exactly what it says, curated for restraint and whitespace
- Land-book (https://land-book.com) — focused on landing pages, useful for hero section inspiration
