## What It Is

Massive, searchable library of real-world app and web design patterns. Over 1,000 iOS & Web apps, 200 sites, 606,500 screens, and 323,900 user flows. New content added weekly.

## Key Features

- **Search by pattern type**: Screens (Profile, Wallet, Login, etc.), UI Elements, Flows, or Text in Screenshot
- **Interactive flows**: Experience user journeys with transitions, micro-interactions, and animations — or step through in prototype mode
- **Figma plugin**: Copy designs straight into Figma
- **Collections**: Save favorites and upload your own screenshots
- **Comments**: Take notes so you remember context later

## Why Useful

Trusted by design teams at Coinbase, Wise, Headspace, Airbnb, Uber, Nike, Pinterest, Shopify, Notion, Dropbox, and more. Saves research time by showing how world-class products handle specific UI patterns and flows. Great for establishing best practices and standards.

Free tier available; paid plans for full access.
