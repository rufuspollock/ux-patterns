Curated galleries — highest signal-to-noise

- Godly (godly.website) — probably the best single source right now for genuinely beautiful, unusual sites. Curated by taste, not popularity.
- Awwwards (awwwards.com) — longer-running, more well-known, slightly more maximalist/award-bait tendency but still excellent
- Siteinspire (siteinspire.com) — older, calmer curation, skews toward the clean and typographic. Good for the Flowershow register specifically.
- Httpster (httpster.net) — smaller, quirkier, good for finding things off the beaten path

More specific:

- Minimal Gallery (minimal.gallery) — exactly what it says, curated for restraint and whitespace
- Land-book (land-book.com) — focused on landing pages, useful for hero section inspiration
